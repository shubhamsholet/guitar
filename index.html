Strum and Play: Experience the Interactive Guitar!

I've crafted an interactive guitar using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP. This virtual instrument lets you play notes and features animated strings that respond to your strumming, offering a realistic touch. With smooth animations and responsive design, it provides an engaging musical experience right in your browser. Click on the strings or fretboard to create different melodies and chords. Dive in and enjoy the harmonious blend of technology and music, bringing out the musician in you!
